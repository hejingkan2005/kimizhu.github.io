Learn How to Mod Mech Commander 2: 3-Part Webcast

Dan Fernandez - MSFT 5/1/2006 9:59:00 PM

As I blogged about earlier, and as was mentioned on Slashdot and Digg, Mech Commander 2 is available to download, and using Visual C++ 2005 Express Edition, and the webcasts listed below, you too can learn how to create your own game mods.

Michael Kulcher, a PM on the XNA team is doing a 3-part webcast (more info) on how to create your own mods so mark your calendars for the week of May 26th!

  • MSDN Webcast: MechCommander 2 Mods (Part 1 of 3) (Level 100)
    Date:   Monday, May 22, 2006 1:00 PM Pacific Time (US & Canada)
  • MSDN Webcast: MechCommander 2 Mods (Part 2 of 3) (Level 100)
    Date:   Wednesday, May 24, 2006 1:00 PM Pacific Time (US & Canada)
  • MSDN Webcast: MechCommander 2 Mods (Part 3 of 3) (Level 100)
    Date:   Friday, May 26, 2006 1:00 PM Pacific Time (US & Canada)
