Still catching up on old email, but I did want to post that Windows Developer Power Tools was published last Friday, congrats to Jim Holmes and James Avery on pulling together a great collection of tools (170+ and over 1,300 pages). I personally authored the Visual Studio Express Chapter so I guess I can consider myself a writer now, and frankly I'm in good company in the book with Rob Howard, Sara Ford, Adam Nathan, and many others contributing.  James has a blog entry to wet your appetite on some of the tools, you can check out a sample chapter on O'Reilly Books, read an interview with Jim and James and of course order through Amazon.
