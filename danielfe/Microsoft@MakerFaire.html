Microsoft @ Maker Faire

Dan Fernandez - MSFT 5/12/2007 9:15:06 PM

Jacqueline Russell has been planning another great presence at this year's Maker Faire and I have to say I'm pretty pumped! Check out the Maker Faire Coding4Fun page for full details. 

 

Microsoft will be out in full force including: Windows Home Server, Windows Live Spaces, Microsoft Robotics Studio, XNA, SQL Server, Visual Studio, Microsoft Academic team, Evangelism including Channel9 and on10, Virtual Earth, Microsoft Research, and even two of our Coding4Fun authors, Clint Rutkas and Brian Peek.

 

If you're in San Francisco May 19th & 20th, spend $15 and go visit what I consider to be the largest science fair/experiment in the world.
