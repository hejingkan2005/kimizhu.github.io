Connections in Vegas

Dan Fernandez - MSFT 11/9/2004 5:44:00 PM

I'm in Vegas this week for Connections. Connections is a 3-part conference covering ASP.NET, Visual Studio, and SQL Server.  I did the demo keynote for Marie Huwe, our fearless leader, and a session on language and IDE enhancements. I'm happy to be done with sessions. All the usual suspects are here, Juval Lowy and Carl Franklin, the conference chairs always get good speakers. Scott Guthrie, Rob Howard, Julie Lerman, Tom Rizzo, Goldfarb, Prashant, Mark Michaelis, Bill Evjen, Doug Seven, Ari Bixhorn, and even Rory.

The keynote went well and the demo didn't die. I'll go into more detail on the keynote and my session a little later.
